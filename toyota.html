Toyota Fortuner

The Toyota Fortuner was first introduced in 2004, as a part of Toyota's IMV (Innovative International Multi-purpose Vehicle) project. With over 1 million units sold worldwide, the Fortuner has established itself as a leading player in the SUV market.
